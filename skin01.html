About Us

NewsPurse is a technology partner to digital media organizations. We bring revenue and marketing opportunities to digital content providers through our digital pass.

Who We are

Our vision is to enable journalism and creativity to prosper in the digital marketplace. Research shows that consumers are willing to pay for quality news, features and content, but the dynamics of the marketplace are shifting and disrupting traditional revenue sources. As consumption shifts from print to digital, consumption patterns are changing as well. NewsPurse was founded in 2013 to address this change. Our digital pass works across multiple providers giving publishers new revenue opportunities and consumers choice.
